A funny story

"Chipper"

At one of the work parties, when still living in Ireland, when I approached a group of colleagues, they were in the middle of talking about the best "Chippers". Being an introvert, I hardly ever speak in crowds, but decided that this is my moment, as just a couple of weeks ago we hired a stripper (chippendale) for my friends bachelorette party. I thought that's a bit awkward thing to talk about on a work party, but then again, Irish are very open and like to have a laugh, so I went along with it... me: "Oh, we had a chipper on my friends bachelorette party recently, but he wasn't really any good. He was dressed as a police man so that was cool, but..."; And then I saw the look on everyone's face... and then my face became red... me: "Isn't that what 'Chippers' are?"; my boss: "Actually 'Chippers' are the ones who are selling you 'Fish and Chips'".

Moral of the story:

If you talk about "Chippers" with your boss, make sure you know what it means first.
